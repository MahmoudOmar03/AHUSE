What AUHSE does for your team

A unified platform for inspection, detection, assessment, and reporting — built for real construction workflows.

AI Hazard Detection

YOLOv8 object detection identifies safety risks — missing PPE, exposed hazards, and non-compliance — directly from your site photos.

Risk Assessment

Automated likelihood × severity calculation with a structured risk matrix, following construction industry standards.

Compliance Reports

Generate professional DOCX reports with hazard descriptions, control measures, and recommendations — ready for audits and filing.

Inspection Archive & History

Every inspection is saved with full metadata — project, date, status, risk level, and downloadable reports. Search, filter, and retrieve any past inspection instantly.

Photo Upload & Export

Drag-and-drop site photos, get structured results, and download reports in DOCX or JSON format.

Team & Verification

Track who inspected, who verified, and when. Built-in accountability for every field report your team generates.

How it works

From photo to compliance report in four simple steps.

Step 01

Create or select a project

Enter project name, site location, and inspector details. Projects are saved for future inspections.

Step 02

Upload a site photo

Upload site images (up to 20 files, 100 MB combined) or video (up to 250 MB), depending on mode. JPEG, PNG, WebP; MP4, WebM, MOV, and other common formats.

Step 03

Review AI-detected hazards

The system identifies hazards, assesses risk using a likelihood × severity matrix, and recommends controls.

Step 04

Download the HSE report

Download a professional compliance-ready DOCX report. Every inspection is saved to your history for future reference.
